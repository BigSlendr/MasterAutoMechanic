Our Story

About Master Auto Mechanic

Master Auto Mechanic is a locally owned and operated auto repair shop serving Staten Island, NY. We are not a chain and we are not a franchise — we are mechanics who live and work in this community.

We have built a reputation for doing the job right and treating customers with respect. You will never see charges on your bill that were not discussed first.

Our shop handles most makes and models — American, European, Asian, older classics, and newer vehicles with modern electronics.

We work on everything from routine oil changes and NY State inspections to brake jobs, diagnostics, A/C service, transmission work, and general repairs.
